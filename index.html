About Usclose

We are here to make you hungry for donuts 🍩, share pointless but awesome facts about doughnuts 🍩, and, more importantly, connect everyone who wants to talk about donuts 🍩. Becuase they're really awesome!


You should join us all the time, 🙃 especially on our National Donut Days: the first of which is National Donut Day, held on the first Friday of June. Established in 1938 to celebrate the Salvation Army Workers, aka Doughnut Girls, who supplied free donuts to American Troops during WWI. On the other hand, our second day is National Doughnut Appreciation Day held annually on November 5th. This day has no rhyme or reason other than a ploy to sell more goods, but it is still a good reason to eat a donut 🍩


The Go Nutz Donutz team is comprised of four hardworking MERN full-stack web development students whose common goals inspired the creation of this website. Contact information can be found in the page footer.
